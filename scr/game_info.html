Silent Hill 3

Silent Hill 3 is a 2003 survival horror game released for the PS2. It is a direct sequel to the first installment in the franchise starring Heather Mason, a teenager who becomes entangled in the machinations of Silent Hill's cult, which seeks to revive a malevolent deity.
